The bond lobby is behind the Jail, 12130 Fillmore Street (across the driveway). Use driveway F, press buzzer at the gate. Drive around back of building to bond lobby. Press buzzer on the door.

Visits occur at the Jail, 12130 Fillmore Street, across the driveway. Parking is available from Driveway D. Check in at the Jail front desk.
